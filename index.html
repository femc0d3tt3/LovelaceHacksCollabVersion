About Her

SHE's bold...
SHE's fierce...
SHE's ready to claim her space.

HER START is an upcoming female-oriented tech platform intended for college students and graduates. We provide tech mentorship, networking, and career opportunities for women around the world.

Her Mentorship

“While we may be individually strong, we are collectively powerful.” -Rakhi Voria, Microsoft

Gaining career advice from female tech-mentors to reach your full potential. Having one-on-one monthly virtual meetings with a mentor. Information on mentor status will be provided in an email confirmation.

Please join our monthly Her Social Mixers, Her Tech Workshops, and Her Tech Seminars. This information will be posted via social media and email newsletters.

  • Women in Data Science -
    • "​The WiDS initiative aims to inspire and educate data scientists worldwide, regardless of gender, and to support women in the field. "
  • Women in Technology -
    • "When one woman helps another, amazing things can happen. Professional careers leap forward. That's what Women in Technology is all about...

    No matter where you are in your professional development, or what technology-related field you're in, our community offers a broad range of support, programs and resources to advance women in technology from the classroom to the boardroom."
  • Women Who Code -
    • "We envision a world where women are proportionally represented as technical leaders, executives, founders, VCs, board members, and software engineers."

For Your Safety:

  • National Domestic Violence Hotline

    • Our advocates are here to listen without judgement and help you begin to address what’s going on in your relationship.
    Our services are always free and available 24/7.
    Call: 800.799.SAFE (7233)

  • 24 Hour Helplines

    • Our 24-Hour helplines are available for over the phone crisis services.
    24-Hour Helplines:
    Domestic Violence | (209) 465-4878 Sexual Assault | (209) 465-4997 Youth/Human Trafficking | (209) 948-1911
    For TTY callers, please dial 711 for assistance.

  • Sexual Assault Crisis

    • WEAVE is the primary provider of crisis intervention services for survivors of domestic violence and sexual assault in Sacramento County. Through a multi-disciplinary effort with Sacramento County, law enforcement, the Commercially Sexually Exploited Children Court, Child Protective Services, and the District Attorney’s Office, WEAVE also provides 24/7 response, outreach and services for victims of sex trafficking.
